Before You Start

Arches requires a 15 card deck for each player, all of one suit. Thirteen of the cards should have numeric values from 1 to 13, plus two “Arches” which represent an overwhelmingly negative or positive outcome. If you’re using a standard deck of playing cards, you can make the ace card a “off-arche” and the king card the “on-arche”, for a deck of thirteen cards in total, which alters the probabilities, but should still be playable.
